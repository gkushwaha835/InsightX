Is InsightX really free?

+

+

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your files are processed securely and not stored permanently on our servers.

Not at all. If you can upload a file, you can use InsightX. Our interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, with step-by-step guides to help you get started.

Your privacy is critically important to us. InsightX only transiently processes your uploaded Amazon reports to generate your real-time analytics dashboard. We never retain, sell, or cross-reference your campaign data with any third parties.

Terms & Conditions

By using InsightX, you agree to leverage our analytics dashboard exclusively for your own Amazon Seller or Agency accounts. InsightX is a free utility tool provided "as-is", and we do not guarantee specific advertising ROAS returns as market conditions vary.

Cookie Policy

We use strictly necessary cookies to keep your session secure while you view your reports. We also utilize minimal performance cookies to understand how our users navigate the dashboard. You can opt out at any time through your browser settings.

What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is the toughest privacy and security law in the world, drafted by the European Union. InsightX is fully GDPR compliant. We allow any user to instantly delete their account and eradicate any trace of data from our analytical processing engines upon request.
