How to download Ads data
  1. Go to Amazon Ads dashboard.
  2. In left sidebar click on Bulk Operation.
  3. Select date range (Month) you want to analyze.
  4. Tick only Sponsored Product, Sponsored Display, Sponsored Brand.
  5. Click Create Spreadsheet and wait until it downloads.
How to download Organic data
  1. Go to Seller Central > Business Report.
  2. In left side click on Detail Page Sales and Traffic.
  3. Select date range and click download.
